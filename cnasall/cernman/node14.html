The crngeant style

The crngeant  minor style includes all the commands of cernman  and cernlib . As the layout for the description of the individual routines in the GEANT manual is somewhat different from the one of the CERNLIB short writeups, a different command, \Makehead (instead of \Cernhead) should be used:

\Makehead{Heading title}

An example of the beginning of the description of GEANT routine BASE020 is shown below. 

\Authors {F.Bruyant,M.Maire}  \Origin{GEANT3}
\Submitted{01.10.84}          \Revised{23.05.91}
\Version{Geant 3.11}          \Routid{BASE020}
