Special minor styles

The CERNLIB and GEANT manuals have a somewhat special layout, since they contain many (over 200) more or less short descriptions of independent routines. Therefore a special macro-command was defined in each case to format these manual pages, tagging the relevant information for each routine in an adequate way. These specific commands are grouped in two minor styles, cernlib  and crngeant , which are described below.
