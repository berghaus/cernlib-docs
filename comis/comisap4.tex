\chapter{\COMIS{} source files and libraries}
 
On the CERN machines, \COMIS{} is provided in the context of PAW.

\subsection*{IBM}

\begin{alltt} 
\Ucom{CERNLIB PAWLIB}
\Ucom{LOAD user (NOAUTO}
\end{alltt} 

\subsection*{VAX}
 
\begin{alltt}
\Ucom{CERNLIB PAWLIB}
\Ucom{LINK user,'LIB\$'}
\end{alltt}

\subsection*{Unix}
 
\begin{alltt}
\Ucom{f77 user.o `cernlib pawlib`}
\end{alltt}

\subsection*{Apollo}
 
\begin{alltt}
\Ucom{ld user.bin `cernlib pawlib`}
\end{alltt}
